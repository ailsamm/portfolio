PRODUCTIFY

Productify is a full-stack ReactJS app and your new favorite tool for organizing projects and increasing productivity. Designed with a Kanban-style interface, its Postgresql / NodeJS backend structure securely persists data from one session to the next. Responsive ChartJS animations give quick and efficient visual cues of a project's progress to the user.

MOODI

Moodi is a full-stack versatile ReactJS app that takes the effort out of keeping a mood journal all while allowing you to track your mood and the different factors that contribute to your ups and downs. It lets you quickly take note of how you felt during a particular day through the use of quick-tap buttons and analyse your mood history via stats and figures in your personalized dashboard with ChartJS animations. The Postgresql / NodeJS backend securely and smoothly persists data.

PLANNIT

Say goodbye to multiple open tabs and trawling across the web for hours on end – with Plannit, simply enter where you'd like to go and when and you will automatically be provided with every detail you need to plan your trip, from flights to accommodations, restaurants to activities and even a weather forecast for your destination. Built purely with JavaScript, jQuery and HTML5/ CSS3, this lightweight app is the best friend you never knew you needed!
